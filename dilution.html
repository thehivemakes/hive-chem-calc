The Dilution Equation: C1V1 = C2V2

The dilution equation C1V1 = C2V2 is used to determine how to dilute a concentrated stock solution to a desired final concentration and volume.

C1 is the initial concentration, V1 is the volume of stock solution to use, C2 is the final desired concentration, and V2 is the final total volume. Solve for any one unknown given the other three.

To make the dilution: pipette V1 of stock solution into a vessel, then add solvent (V2 − V1) to reach the final volume V2.
