How to Calculate Molarity

Molarity (M) is the number of moles of solute per liter of solution. It is the most common way to express concentration in chemistry.

To prepare a solution of known molarity: (1) calculate the mass needed using mass = M × V × molar mass, (2) weigh the solute, (3) dissolve in less than the final volume of solvent, (4) dilute to the final volume in a volumetric flask.
