Mole-Gram-Molecule Conversions

Stoichiometry is the calculation of quantities in chemical reactions. The mole is the bridge between the atomic scale and the lab scale.

One mole of any substance contains 6.022 × 10²³ particles (Avogadro’s number) and has a mass in grams equal to its molar mass.
